Description: "Inception" is a mind-bending science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan.

The story revolves around a skilled thief who is capable of entering the dreams of others to steal their secrets.

He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully perform the inverse task - implanting an idea into someone's mind.
