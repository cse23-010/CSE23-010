Description: "The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild" is an action-adventure video game developed and published by Nintendo.

It is set in a vast open world and follows Link, who awakens from a deep slumber to discover that the kingdom of Hyrule has been devastated by the ancient evil entity Calamity Ganon.

Link must explore the kingdom, defeat enemies, solve puzzles, and rescue Princess Zelda to save Hyrule.
